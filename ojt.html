Wastewater includes substances such as human waste, food scraps, oils, soaps and chemicals. Nature has an amazing ability to cope with small amounts of wastewater and pollution, but it would be overwhelmed if we didn't treat the billions of gallons of wastewater and sewage produced every day before releasing it back to the environment.The biggest challenge is about the sludge and the solids. Much of the wastewater used by homes, industries, and businesses must be treated before it is released back to the environment. These solids must be treated and disposed of in an appropriate manner.
